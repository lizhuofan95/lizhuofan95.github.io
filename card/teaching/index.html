Teaching

In addition to teaching in my substantive and methodological specialties, I am a strong proponent for proactively preparing sociologists for the future convergence, as my own reseasrch suggests, (a) of social science and computer science and (b) of industry and academic careers.

Relevant training can be integrated into the existing intro-theory-stats-methods sequence at both undergraduate and graduate levels:

  1. Sociological theory of big data and for big data
  2. Computational literacy and toolkits for social scientists
  3. Hidden curriculum in open science, team science, data collection and management, interdisciplinarity, and industry careers

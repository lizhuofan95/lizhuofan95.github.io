In addition to teaching in my substantive and methodological specialties, I am a strong proponent for proactively preparing sociologists for leading and criticizing future convergence between social and computational sciences and deepened corporate participation in social science. Relevant training can be integrated into the existing intro-theory-stats-methods sequence at both undergraduate and graduate levels:
1. Sociological theory for and of big data
2. Computational repertoire for all sociologists
3. An interdisciplinary foundation in data management, digital privacy, open science, and corporate social science
